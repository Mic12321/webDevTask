    Rubik's cube Tutorial

    Rubik's cube Tutorial

    4 x 4 cubes Tutorial

    (Click the 4x4 cube picture)

    This 4 x 4 cube tutorial provided very detail formulas and ideas to solve the advanced cubes. Once you know how to solve 4 x 4 cubes. You can solve 5 x 5 cubes and more advanced cubes. The logic and ideas are same to them.

    Notation R

    The surface that you are facing. Turn the first right column to the top.

    Notation R'

    The surface that you are facing. Turn the first right column to the bottom.

    Notation F

    The surface that you are facing. Turn the whole surface clockwise 90 degree.

    Notation F'

    The surface that you are facing. Turn the whole surface anti-clockwise 90 degree.

    Notation U

    The surface that you are facing. Turn the first top row to left.

    Notation U'

    The surface that you are facing. Turn the first top row to right.

    Notation D

    The surface that you are facing. Turn the bottom row to right.

    Notation D'

    The surface that you are facing. Turn the bottom row to left.

    Notation L

    The surface that you are facing. Turn the left column to the bottom.

    Notation L'

    The surface that you are facing. Turn the left column to the top.

    Notation B

    Turn the back block side to left.

    Notation B'

    Turn the back block side to right.
