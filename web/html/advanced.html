    Rubik's cube Tutorial

    Rubik's cube Tutorial

    Advanced tip 1

    Choose one colour for the base. Usually, white is the best to train. Then do the first side and first layer without flipping around the cube. It can help you save the cube flipping time and increase the speed. If you do lots of practice, you can solve a Rubik's cube in 1 minute.

    Advanced tip 2

    Use a higher tolerance cube allows you to play the cube faster and smoother. It is worth to upgrade your cube and have better play experience.

    Advanced tip 3

    Try to do first two layer together after base cross. You can get the idea in this website. Or if you want to get the formula directly, you can visit this website. After you practice F2L, you can solve a Rubik's cube around 30-40 seconds.

    Advanced tip 4

    To solve a Rubik's cube in 30 seconds, you need to practice and remember a lot of formulas. Including the top and the third layer. For OLL, please visit this website. For PPL, please visit this website.
